Kelechi Ezihie

Founder & CEO

Kelechi is a Nigerian-American golfer, who caddied throughout his youth but truly embraced the game in adulthood and on his own terms — finding joy, purpose, and connection through every swing. What began as a personal journey soon evolved into a mission: to eliminate barriers within the sport and build a vibrant, diverse community around it.

Kelechi is currently attempting a Guinness World Record for the longest golf marathon, with hopes of using the proceeds to build a golf center in Nigeria.
