Learn To Golf

Whether you're new to the game or looking to sharpen your skills, our programs are designed for all ages and skill levels. Explore our lessons, clinics, and camps to find the right fit for your golf journey.

Lessons

One-on-one or small group sessions tailored to individual needs.

  • Instructor: Kelechi Ezihie
  • For who: Beginners, intermediate players, or those seeking personalized instruction.
  • Duration: 30 Minutes, 60 Minutes and multi-session packages available.

Clinics

Skill-specific group training sessions held regularly.

  • Instructor: Kelechi Ezihie
  • For who: Players looking to focus on specific aspects like putting, chipping, driving, etc.

Camps

Multi-day immersive experiences perfect for juniors or group learning.

  • Instructor: Kelechi Ezihie
  • For who: Kids, teens, and adults wanting a more in-depth golf experience.
  • Duration: 30 Minutes, 60 Minutes and multi-session packages available.
